The Day 2 Day

Another season in Spain, but this year, in Gijon. Days seems to fly by here, but thats mostly because of the two practices a day. I sometimes get questions on what I do all day over here. So here is a quick rundown into my regular day in Gijon.

Morning

Surprisingly breakfast isn't a big thing here. Interesting cause all I ever heard growing up was that breakfast is the most important meal. Regardless I try to wake up and eat something simple like oatmeal, toast or eggs. Our first practice is either a 10 o'clock lift or a 10:30 on the court practice. 10:30 practice means ready to go by then, so I try and leave by 9:45am. Practice goes until about 12.. but by the time I finish stretching, cooling down, and showering I get out by about 12:45.

Lunch

We eat lunch together everyday at a restaurant in the city. The three course meal takes a while, so we usually get out around 3:30. After that, I usually take a very neccssary nap. Days with two practices that nap is vital. At least for an hour. Post nap I wake up, try and watch some basketball either a game of ours, a team we will play, or any other basketball games or highlights I can find.

Evening

With a 6:30 practice....(on the court ready to go) I try and leave the apartment again by 5:45-6. Same thing post practice... stretch.. sit in the locker room and talk for a little, then shower. Usually get out the gym around 9. By then I'm almost always starving and trying to figure out what to eat for dinner.

Night

If i have enough energy, I'm making something to eat. If not then I'll buy something fast so I can get home. The night usually consists of Netflix, movies, or reading. That carries on until about 12.. 1. Then the next day the same thing all over again. This is just the usual everyday schedule.. but of course its different during off days or on days we only have one practice as well as game days.
